Tips to Keep Your Passwords Secure
  • Change your password every 3 to 6 months.
  • Use different passwords for different accounts.
  • Enable two-factor authentication where possible.
  • Avoid writing passwords down or storing them in plain text.
  • Be cautious of phishing attempts and avoid clicking on suspicious links.

For more detailed information on keeping your passwords secure, visit the NCSC's Top Tips for Staying Secure Online.
